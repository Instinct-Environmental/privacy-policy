Privacy Policy

Last Updated:4/3/2025

**Privacy Policy** **Last Updated:** 4/3/2025 Instinct Environmental Company ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information related to our use of the camera in our mobile application ("App"). ### **1. Information We Collect** Our App uses the camera solely for the purpose of scanning QR codes. These QR codes contain a **Sensor ID**, which is uploaded to our database along with the user’s **current location**. ### **2. How We Use Your Information** We use the collected Sensor ID and location data to: - Associate sensor readings with a specific location. - Store this information securely in our database for operational and analytical purposes. ### **3. Camera Access** Our App requests **camera permissions** only for QR code scanning. We **do not** record, store, or share images, videos, or any other camera-related data. ### **4. Data Sharing and Security** - We do **not** share your camera data with third parties. - The Sensor ID and location data collected are transmitted securely and stored in compliance with industry security standards. ### **5. User Control** You can disable camera access in your device settings at any time, but this may limit the App’s functionality. ### **6. Changes to This Policy** We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App or on our website. ### **7. Contact Us** If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: https://instinctenvironmental.com.
